## Overview

Implemented a **deep learning system** to assess structural damage in conflict zones and visualize missing infrastructure in 3D.

### Key Features

- **Automated Damage Detection**: AI-powered analysis of structural damage from satellite/aerial imagery
- **3D Visualization**: Reconstruction and visualization of damaged and missing infrastructure
- **Conflict Zone Analysis**: Specialized for assessment in challenging environments
- **Change Detection**: Identifies structural changes over time

### Technologies Used

- Deep Learning / Computer Vision
- 3D Reconstruction
- Satellite Imagery Analysis
- Change Detection Algorithms
