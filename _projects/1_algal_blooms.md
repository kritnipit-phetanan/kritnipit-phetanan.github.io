## Overview

Developed a predictive **Faster R-CNN** model for algal blooms and trace contaminants in water systems to enable early warning capabilities.

### Key Features

- **Deep Learning Detection**: Utilized Faster R-CNN architecture for accurate detection of algal bloom patterns
- **Multi-contaminant Analysis**: Capable of identifying various trace contaminants in water systems
- **Early Warning System**: Enables proactive response to potential water quality issues
- **Remote Sensing Integration**: Leverages satellite and aerial imagery for large-scale monitoring

### Technologies Used

- Python, PyTorch
- Faster R-CNN
- Remote Sensing / Satellite Imagery
- GIS Analysis
